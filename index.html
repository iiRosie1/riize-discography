RISE & REALIZE

RIIZE (pronounced as "Rise") is a South Korean boy band formed by SM Entertainment. They debuted on September 4, 2023, with the single album Get a Guitar.

The group's name, RIIZE, is a name created by combining "Rise", and "Realize", which means "a team that rises together and realizes dreams”.

The fanclub’s name, BRIIZE means that, like a breeze blowing everywhere, we will help RIIZE soar and be with RIIZE anytime, anywhere.
